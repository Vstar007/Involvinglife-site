Program Name & Description

The FirstMinuteReply Lead Response Program sends automated text messages on behalf of participating HVAC and plumbing contractors. When a homeowner submits a service request, they receive a confirmation text message that the request was received, and may receive follow-up messages regarding that request.

Consent

By submitting a service request form that includes your phone number, you consent to receive text messages related to your service inquiry. Consent is not a condition of purchase.

Message Frequency

Message frequency varies based on your service request — typically 1–3 messages per inquiry (a confirmation and follow-up).

Message & Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply according to your mobile carrier plan.

Opting Out

You can cancel text messages at any time by replying STOP. After you send STOP, you will receive a final message confirming you have been unsubscribed, and no further messages will be sent to your number.

Help & Support

For assistance, reply HELP to any message, or contact us at hello@involvinglife.com.
